Shivani Lakshman

Engineer | Singer | Sportswoman

Shivani is passionate about Science and Music and aspires to pursue a career in both these streams. Her fascination for Science started right from her childhood, thanks to her grandfather, an electrical engineer who fuelled her with interesting concepts in science. Shivani was also enrolled in carnatic music classes and has started to perform concerts from 2018. She is under the tutelage of Sri. C. R. Vaidyanathan, one of the senior-most disciples of Sri. P. S. Narayanaswamy.
